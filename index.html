G'day, I'm Crusty!

With over 35 years of professionally falling over, honking horns, and making children scream (with laughter — mostly), Crusty the Clown is the go-to entertainer for birthdays, corporate events, school fairs, and the occasional existential crisis.

Trained at the International Institute of Slapstick in Vienna, Crusty holds a Diploma in Advanced Pratfalls and a certificate in Responsible Confetti Use.
